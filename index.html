About The Hut

Mini Pancake Hut is owned by husband and wife duo. Our small business founded in Humble, Texas in 2022 by Irene Garibay and her husband Ben. It focuses specifically in catering delicious fluffy delights such as our premiere mini pancakes and refreshing iced coffee. We pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service and ensure grade A quality all of the time.

Our staff are trained professionals and every plate served is nicely decorated to the customers request. We ensure a 100% satisfaction guarantee with our services, our detailed set up, and our delicious mini pancakes! Every event we have done is with love, joy and an open heart. Pancake making is not only our job but, it is our passion.

Seeing our customers and their guest enjoying our mini pancakes and delicious sweets satisfies our hearts with every delicious bite!

Our Service

  • 2.5 Hour Service: We provide a full two and a half hours of live pancake making.
  • Unlimited Pancakes: Your guests can enjoy as many fluffy delights as they wish within the service time.
  • Professional Staff: Includes 2-3 friendly staff members, depending on your guest count.
  • Custom Menu: You can choose 5 toppings and 5 drizzles from our menu for your event.
